About

Enough fights, enough arguments, end all discussions by rolling the dice! Sit with your partner, pick a die each, hit the button and see who wins. It's as simple as that!

This site is an application manipulating the DOM with pure vanilla Javascript, using random number generation with Math.Random() and Math.Floor() methods, along with a delayed loop to make visible dice image swaps before showing the final result.
